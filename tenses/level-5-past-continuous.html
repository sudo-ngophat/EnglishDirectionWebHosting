Level 5 — Past Continuous

Past Continuous là gì?

Dùng để nói về hành động đang xảy ra tại một thời điểm trong quá khứ:

  • Đang xảy ra tại 1 thời điểm: At 8 PM last night, I was watching TV.
  • Hành động bị ngắt bởi hành động khác: I was sleeping when the phone rang.
  • 2 hành động xảy ra song song: She was cooking while he was cleaning.
  • Bối cảnh cho câu chuyện: The sun was shining and birds were singing.
(+) S + was/were + V-ing    →   She was working.
(-) S + was/were + not + V-ing    →   She was not working.
(?) Was/Were + S + V-ing?    →   Was she working?
was vs were:
• I / he / she / it → was
• you / we / they → were

Past Simple vs Past Continuous

Past SimplePast Continuous
Hành động ngắn, hoàn tất
The phone rang.		Hành động dài, đang diễn ra
I was sleeping.
Chuỗi sự kiện nối tiếp
He came in and sat down.		Bối cảnh / hành động song song
It was raining and people were running.
Công thức kinh điển: Past Continuous + when + Past Simple
I was walking home when it started to rain.
(đang đi → trời bắt đầu mưa = hành động dài bị ngắt bởi hành động ngắn)

While + Past Continuous, Past Simple
While I was cooking, the doorbell rang.
