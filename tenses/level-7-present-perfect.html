Level 7 — Present Perfect

Present Perfect là gì?

Dùng khi hành động đã xảy ra nhưng còn liên quan đến hiện tại:

  • Kinh nghiệm (đã từng): I have visited Japan twice.
  • Kết quả còn ảnh hưởng: She has lost her keys. (= bây giờ không có chìa khóa)
  • Từ quá khứ đến hiện tại (for/since): I have lived here for 5 years.
  • Vừa mới xảy ra (just/already/yet): He has just arrived.
(+) S + have/has + V3 (past participle)    →   She has gone home.
(-) S + have/has + not + V3    →   She has not gone home.
(?) Have/Has + S + V3?    →   Has she gone home?
have vs has:
• I / you / we / they → have ('ve)
• he / she / it → has ('s)

Present Perfect vs Past Simple

Present PerfectPast Simple
Không nói thời gian cụ thể
I have been to Paris.		Có thời gian cụ thể
I went to Paris last year.
Còn liên quan đến hiện tại
I have lost my phone. (= vẫn chưa tìm thấy)		Đã kết thúc hoàn toàn
I lost my phone yesterday. (= chuyện đã qua)
for / since / ever / never / just / already / yet
She has worked here since 2020.		yesterday / last week / ago / in 2020
She started working here in 2020.
Mẹo: Nếu câu có "yesterday, last week, ago, in 2015" → dùng Past Simple.
Nếu câu có "ever, never, just, already, yet, for, since" → dùng Present Perfect.

V3 (Past Participle) thường gặp

go → gone • see → seen • do → done • have → had • be → been
eat → eaten • write → written • take → taken • give → given • know → known
break → broken • speak → spoken • drive → driven • forget → forgotten
buy → bought • bring → brought • think → thought • find → found
Quy tắc: worked, played, studied, lived (giống V2)
