Modal Verbs

can / could / should / must / may / might / would

Beginner — Cơ bản
1
Can / Could / Be able to
Khả năng, xin phép, yêu cầu. "I can swim." "Could you help me?"
Beginner
2
Must / Should / Have to
Bắt buộc, khuyên nhủ, cần thiết. "You must stop." "You should rest."
Intermediate
3
May / Might / Will / Would
Khả năng xảy ra, xin phép formal, đề nghị lịch sự
Intermediate
4
Mixed Modals — Boss Level
Tổng hợp + Modal Perfect (should have done, could have been...)
Advanced

Modal verbs không chia (không thêm s/es), luôn đi với V nguyên mẫu, không dùng "to" sau modal.
