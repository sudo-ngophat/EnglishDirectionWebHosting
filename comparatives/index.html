Comparatives & Superlatives

So sánh hơn & So sánh nhất

1
Comparatives — So sánh hơn
bigger, more expensive, better, worse + than
Beginner
2
Superlatives — So sánh nhất
the biggest, the most expensive, the best, the worst
Intermediate
3
Mixed + Special Patterns
as...as, the more...the more, less/fewer, bất quy tắc
Advanced

Quy tắc: từ ngắn (1 âm tiết) → thêm -er/-est. Từ dài (2+ âm tiết) → more/most. Bất quy tắc: good-better-best, bad-worse-worst.
