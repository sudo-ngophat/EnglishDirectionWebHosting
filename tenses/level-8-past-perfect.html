Level 8 — Past Perfect

Past Perfect là gì?

Dùng để nói về hành động đã xảy ra TRƯỚC một hành động khác trong quá khứ:

Had + V3 (xảy ra trước) → rồi → Past Simple (xảy ra sau) → NOW
  • Hành động xảy ra trước: When I arrived, the movie had already started.
  • Kinh nghiệm trước 1 mốc quá khứ: She had never seen snow before she moved to Canada.
  • Nguyên nhân trong quá khứ: He failed the exam because he had not studied.
(+) S + had + V3    →   She had left before I arrived.
(-) S + had not + V3    →   She had not left yet.
(?) Had + S + V3?    →   Had she left before you arrived?
Khi nào PHẢI dùng Past Perfect?
• Khi muốn nhấn mạnh thứ tự: hành động nào xảy ra TRƯỚC
• Thường đi với: before, after, when, by the time, already, just, never... before

Khi nào KHÔNG cần?
• Nếu thứ tự đã rõ ràng (dùng "then", "and then"), Past Simple là đủ:
   I woke up, brushed my teeth, and left. (thứ tự rõ → không cần Past Perfect)

Dấu hiệu nhận biết

before, after, when, by the time, already, just, never... before, until, as soon as

Ví dụ:

  • By the time I got there, the train had left.
  • She had never traveled abroad before she turned 30.
  • After he had finished dinner, he watched TV.
