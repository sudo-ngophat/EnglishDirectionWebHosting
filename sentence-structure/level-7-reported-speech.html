Level 7 — Reported Speech

Câu tường thuật là gì?

Dùng để thuật lại lời người khác nói mà không trích dẫn trực tiếp.

Direct: She said, "I am tired."
Reported: She said (that) she was tired.

Direct: He said, "I will help you."
Reported: He said (that) he would help me.

Câu hỏi tường thuật:
• Yes/No question → if/whether: "Are you happy?" → He asked if I was happy.
• Wh- question → giữ wh-: "Where do you live?" → He asked where I lived.
• Bỏ dấu hỏi, đổi sang trật tự câu trần thuật (S + V, không đảo)
