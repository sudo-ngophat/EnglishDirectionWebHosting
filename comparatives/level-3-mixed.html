Level 3 — Mixed BOSS

Cấu trúc so sánh đặc biệt

as...as — bằng nhau:
   She is as tall as her mother. / not as expensive as I thought.

the more...the more — càng...càng:
   The more you practice, the better you get.
   The harder you work, the more successful you become.

less / fewer — ít hơn:
   less + không đếm được: less money, less time
   fewer + đếm được: fewer people, fewer books

much / far / a lot + comparative — nhấn mạnh:
   She is much taller than me. / This is far more expensive.
Bất quy tắc đầy đủ:
good → better → the best
bad → worse → the worst
far → farther/further → the farthest/furthest
little → less → the least
many/much → more → the most
old → older/elder → the oldest/eldest
